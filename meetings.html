Subscribe to icalendar feed of upcoming teleconferences

Fortnightly 60 minute teleconference Thursdays, at 9am US Eastern Time (specific schedule mantained in the group teleconferences icalendar) on WebEx bridge which can be joined either on your computer or by phone - see details for joining the call. We will continue to use zakim to manage the queue (q+, q-, q? etc) but it is not integrated with WebEx. For more information see the WebEx best practices.

IRC channel #dap on irc.w3.org port 6665 (Web IRC client).

Please note that attendance of teleconferences is restricted to registered WG participants and persons invited by the chairs. On each call please register attendance in IRC using:

Present+ firstName_lastName

We're tracking action items using Tracker. You may see your actions using MyTracker.

Upon completing actions, please move them to the Pending status, and inform the WG with a summary of the action results, (including ACTION-# in the body of the message so it is linked to the action record). Text lists of pending actions and open actions are also available.
